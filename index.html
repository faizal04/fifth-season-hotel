about us

Welcome to Hotel Fifth Season, located in the beautiful valley of Pahalgam. Our hotel is Centrally heated to ensure that you stay cozy and comfortable, even in the coldest of seasons. We offer 19 rooms in three categories: Super Deluxe, Family Suites, and Deluxe Rooms. Each room is designed to provide you with the utmost comfort and luxury and is equipped with modern amenities to cater your every need.

Our beautiful dining hall offers a delicious variety of cuisines, prepared by our expert chefs who use only the freshest ingredients. At Hotel Fifth Season, we believe in providing an all-encompassing experience that will leave you with memories to cherish for a lifetime. Located just 90km away from Srinagar, our hotel is easily accessible, making it the perfect destination for a weekend getaway or a long vacation. Our tagline, "Experience the Fifth Season of Luxury and Comfort," captures our commitment to providing you with an exceptional stay that goes beyond just a comfortable room.

frequently asked questions

What amenities does the hotel offer?

The hotel offers a range of amenities, including a restaurant, free Wi-Fi, room service, Private Garden and Non-Smoking Rooms

Is there parking available at the hotel?

Yes, the hotel offers free parking to its guests.

What adventure options are available for tourists in Pahalgam?

Tourists in Pahalgam can enjoy adventure activities such as white water rafting, trekking, skiing, snow-sledging, and angling.

What are some popular tourist attractions in Pahalgam?

Some popular tourist attractions in Pahalgam include the Betaab Valley, Chandanwari, Aru Valley, Mamleshwar Temple, and the Amarnath Yatra.

Are orthopedic beds available at your hotel?

Yes, our hotel offers orthopedic beds designed to provide proper spinal support and comfortable sleep.
